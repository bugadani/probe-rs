The ARM AP registers (`CSW`, `IDR`, `BASE`, ...) generated by `define_ap_register!` are now `bitfield!` newtypes over the raw `u32` instead of plain structs with public fields. Field access moves from `reg.Field` / `reg.Field = x` to the `reg.Field()` / `reg.set_Field(x)` accessors. Enum-backed fields keep infallible getters: an encoding that isn't a known variant is returned as the enum's new `Unknown(bits)` variant rather than failing to parse. This removes the hand-written, duplicated bit-packing in each register's `from`/`to` closures.
